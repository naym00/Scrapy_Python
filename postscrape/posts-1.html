      Top tips for finding perfect Hyderabad hotel deals

      • If youre looking for a cheap hotel in Hyderabad, you should consider visiting during the low season. You'll find cheaper accommodations in Hyderabad in April and March.
      • Hotel room prices vary depending on many factors but youll most likely find the best hotel deals in Hyderabad if you stay on a Saturday. The opposite is true for, Monday, which is usually the most expensive day.
      • Book at least 89 days before your stay begins to get the best price for your Hyderabad accommodation.
      • The cheapest 3-star hotel room in Hyderabad found on KAYAK in the last 2 weeks was 1,004, while the most expensive was 1,957.
      • How long should you stay? KAYAK users usually book their accommodation in Hyderabad for 1 day.
      • For those short-stay travellers to Hyderabad there are convenient hotels choices close to Rajiv Gandhi International Airport. Novotel Hyderabad Airport is a short 2.6 km away and features a free pool, parking and airport shuttle while OYO 7627 Royal Plaza is situated just 4.8 km from the airport with free breakfast and Wi-Fi.
      • If youre visiting Hyderabad and prefer to stay in the Old City, Hotel NAV Bharat lies within Old Circle 2, close to the town centre and 1.9 km from the Charminar district. Or if you prefer, Hotel Vaishnaoi can be found in Old Circle 3, featuring a bar/lounge, restaurant and room service and is 3.8 km from Charminar.
      • For those travelling to Hyderabad on business, the Trident Hyderabad is conveniently located in HITEC City financial area with a 24-hour business centre and conference rooms. Similarly, the Hyderabad Marriott Hotel & Convention Centre is within the business centre overlooking the Hussain Sagar Lake. Full convention, banquet and meeting room facilities are available.
      • If you prefer more luxurious accommodations in Hyderabad, the deluxe Taj Krishna can be found 1.1 km from Banjara Hills, offering yoga classes, personal trainer and barber & beauty shop services. Alternatively, the Avasa Hotel is a 5-star luxury hotel some 3.5 km from Jubilee Hills and boasts its own nightclub, 24-hour concierge service and sauna facilities.
      • Sightseers who prefer close proximity to Hyderabads historic cultural centre can relax and enjoy the pool and fitness centre at Luminous One Continent hotel which is just 1.2 km from Lumbini Park. Or you can try the Grand Elite Hotel about 1.7 km from Birla Mandir Temple which has a 24-hour front desk, parking and laundry services.

      • KAYAK users like staying at Park Hyatt Hyderabad, Trident Hyderabad and Novotel Hyderabad Convention Centre when looking for a hotel in close proximity to Charminar.

      • Located 5 km from the Mrugavani National Park is Golkonda Resorts and Spa which has pool and tennis facilities as well as free parking. Alternatively, Park Hyatt Hyderabad can be found about 3 km from KBR National Park and also has free parking along with pool and free breakfast.

      • The Westin Hyderabad Mindspace is a brief 0.1 km from Mindspace IT Park and offers the full range of spa treatments including aromatherapy, hydrotherapy and deep tissue massages. Another option is Vivanta By Taj Begumpet located about 1.9 km from Sanjeevaiah Park that features body wraps, scrubs and other treatments for guests.

      • Button Eyes Resort in Tolkatta Village provides easy access to the Dilshuk Shopping area of Hyderabad with its malls and bazaars. It is a family-friendly accommodation and pets are allowed upon request. Alternatively, the Manohar Hyderabad Hotel is 0.04 km from Central Mahal shopping mall in Begumpet with free breakfast, hot tub and bicycle rental amenities.

      • There is a concentration of hotels with easy access to the citys many museums. For example, Hampshire Plaza Hyderabad in Old Circle 5 is 0.5 km from AP State Museum and comes with complimentary breakfast, Wi-Fi and airport shuttle. Then there is the Royalton Hyderabad in Old Circle 6 which stands just 1.4 km from the Salar Jung Museum featuring a bar/lounge, restaurant and 24-hour gym.
